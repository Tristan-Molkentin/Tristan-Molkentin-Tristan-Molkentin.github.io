October 22, 2021

I just arrived at Jupiter, the largest planet in the star system, which is just another name for the heavenly bodies that orbit the sun. I am especially excited to drop down on Jupiter's many moons as their climates can contrast each other greatly. On the journey, I flew through the belt, which is waht people call the strip of asteroids that orbit the sun. Some asteroids created some interesting shapes, but I am glad to be at Jupiter. IMAGE.
